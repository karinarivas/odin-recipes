Banana Bread Recipe

Description of Dish

Banana bread is a semi-sweet bread made with mashes bananas as one of the principle ingredients. Most of the bread's sweetness comes from the healthy sugars naturally present in bananas, so it is considerably healthier than other sweet baked goods that rely on added sugars for their taste.

Ingredients

  • Flour- 1 1/2 cups
  • Sugar- 3/4 cup
  • Salt- 1/2 tsp
  • Baking soda- 1 tsp
  • Vanilla extract- 1/2 tsp
  • Softened butter- 1/2 cup
  • Eggs- 2
  • 3 very ripe bananas
  • Walnuts and raisins add great taste but are not required.- 1 cup of Walnuts

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degree. Grease and flour a bread loaf pan. Lighly roast walnuts on a skillet, continuously stirring so they don't burn. Chop and cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream together 8 Tbsp of softened butter and 3/4 cup of sugar.
  3. Mash bananas with a fork until the consistency of chunky applesauce and add them to the batter along with 2 eggs, mixing until blended.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together: 1 1/2 cups of flour, 1 tsp of baking soda and 1/2 tsp of salt then add to batter.
  5. Add 1/2 tsp of vanilla extract and mix in chopped walnuts and raisins. Pour into prepared loaf pan. Bake at 350ËšF for 55-60 min or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let the banana bread rest for 10 mins before transferring to a wire rack to cool.
